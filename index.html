Build Smart — Section J & J1V3 Compliance, Simplified.

Performance-Based, Energy-Efficient Buildings — Sustainable, Future-Ready & Code-Compliant.

Today’s buildings are more than concrete and walls — they’re living, working environments that must deliver comfort, efficiency and sustainability. Through Section J and J1V3 compliance pathways, we enable you to realise designs that meet rigorous energy-efficiency and greenhouse-gas emissions standards — without compromising on aesthetics or functionality.

Whether you take the standard compliance route or opt for a performance-based J1V3 solution, we ensure your building:

  • meets or exceeds the NCC energy-efficiency requirements, National Construction Code
  • delivers occupant comfort via verified thermal performance standards, National Construction Code
  • achieves optimal long-term energy efficiency, lower running costs and reduced environmental impact.

With expert energy-modelling, glazing/insulation optimisation, and full compliance documentation — we turn complex regulation into design freedom, helping your project deliver lasting value.
